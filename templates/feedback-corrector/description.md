# feedback-corrector

Human-in-the-loop labeling/correction view: shows classified items (emails, tickets, news, training data) with their current label, lets a reviewer flip the label and add a clarification, then assembles a copy-ready correction prompt for the upstream classifier.

**When to use:** Any time you want a human to review and correct AI classifications — email triage feedback, support-ticket routing review, training-data labeling, news categorization, code-review verdicts.
